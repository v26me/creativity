Welcome to the Creativity Study

Thank you for taking part in this study. Your goal is to draw different shapes and to find intersting or beatiful figures.

The figures are composed of connected squares. You start with a blank grid and can add one square at a time if it is connected with the others. Once you reach 10 squares you can save your figure or start to draw again.

Although the rules seem simple, there are many interesting shapes that you could draw. At the last stage of the expirement you will see all the shapes you have saved and would be asked to choose the best among them.

Please spend at least 7-8 minutes on the experiment
